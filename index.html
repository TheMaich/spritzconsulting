Nine years across the production stack. I started in QA, first as Tester and then as Manager at 34BigThings on Redout, then as QA Project Lead at Rebellion on Sniper Elite 4, Strange Brigade and Zombie Army 4. I moved to publishing as Line and Publishing Producer at Raw Fury on Kathy Rain 2, Moonstone Island and Dome Keeper, and most recently I went back in-house as Producer at Untold Games on City 20.

Today I’m a co-founder of Scuola Nash, a game dev school in Turin, I sit on the jury of the Indie Arena Booth at Gamescom, and I run Spritz Consulting, offering production and publishing support to studios that need an experienced hand for a stretch, not a full-time hire.

After all of it, one pattern stands out: what breaks projects is rarely the code or the creative vision. It’s the structure around them. Communication that drifts, scope that quietly doubles, certifications handled too late, pitches that don’t land. That’s where Spritz Consulting comes in. We untangle the work around the work, so your team can focus on the game and actually ship it.
