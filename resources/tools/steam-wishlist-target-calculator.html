Notes
  • Wishlist conversion. Recent data (2024 to 2025) from GameDiscoverCo puts the median Week 1 conversion at 0.15x to 0.17x of launch wishlists for games with 10,000+ wishlists at launch. The 5% and 10% scenarios in this calculator represent the conservative end of the distribution, useful for stress-testing your targets. Cold launches, higher price points, and no demo tend to sit in that range. Strong genre fit, recent Next Fest presence, or an Overwhelmingly Positive rating push conversion higher. Sources: GameDiscoverCo, Oct 2025 and Gamalytic, Apr 2024
  • Wishlists are not customers. A wishlist is a soft signal of intent that decays over time. Convert via launch discount, demo, festival presence, content drops, and visibility windows, not by accumulating wishlists and waiting.
  • Estimation only. Directional indicator, not a forecast you can take to the bank. Real planning needs real data from your store page, your demo, and your wishlist velocity.
